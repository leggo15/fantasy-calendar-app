Item Details:

contract sets:
Three pieces of paper with a contract written on them
page 1 is held by the partnered guild, page 2 is held by the adventuring party, and page 3 is held by the quest issuer.
The adventurer is to write the details of the quest, result desired by the issuer and the agreedupon reward onto page 2.
When the adventurer has signed their copy and the issuer has signed theirs, The written details from page  is transfered to the other two copies and the signatures are trasfered to all copies.

Divine Danger:
A scroll placed just outside of an enterance of any kind which after one minute will ignite. The forusoty of the resulting flames gives insights into the relative danger within.

Discern Weakness:
Reveals what elements the target is weak towards. Can be used as a bonus action.

Discern Strength:
Reveals what elements the target is stong agains. Can be used as a bonus action.

Crumbling Stone Wall:
Creates a stone wall that seal a 5x5 space between 4 surfaces which crumbles after 1 minute or when it has taken 50 dmg.
